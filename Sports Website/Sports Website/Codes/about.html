ABOUT US

"The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (MYAS) is the apex body responsible for promoting and
developing sports and youth affairs in India. With a focus on enhancing the physical and mental well-being
of the nation’s youth, the Ministry works towards providing opportunities for growth and success on both
national and international platforms.

OUR LEADERS

HON’BLE YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS MINISTER

Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya, Union Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports, focuses on promoting grassroots talent through initiatives like Khelo India and Fit India.

HON’BLE YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS MINISTER OF STATE

Mrs. Raksha Nikhil Khadse is the Minister of State for the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports in India. She assists the Union Minister in implementing policies and programs.

HON’BLE YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS SECRETARY OF MINISTER

Mrs. Meeta Rajivlochan is the Secretary of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. She plays a critical administrative role in implementing the Ministry's policies and programs.
