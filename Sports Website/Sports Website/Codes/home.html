Shaping India's Sporting Future

The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports strives to create
world-class athletes, enhance sports infrastructure, and
promote physical fitness across the country.

ABOUT US

The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (MYAS) is the
government body responsible for developing youth and
sports in India. Our mission is to inspire, motivate, and guide
the youth of India to pursue excellence in various fields of
sports and fitness, while promoting a healthy and active
lifestyle across the nation. Through innovative programs and
national initiatives, we aim to foster a culture of
sportsmanship, physical activity, and community
engagement.


Led by the Honorable Minister of Youth Affairs and
Sports, Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya, the Ministry is
committed to creating a sporting ecosystem that
nurtures young talent.
