Proud Moments of
Indian Sports

India has had several proud moments in sports, such as Neeraj Chopra's historic gold in javelin at
the Tokyo 2020 Olympics and the Indian men's cricket team's World Cup victories. PV Sindhu,
Mary Kom, and Hima Das have also made their mark globally, bringing home medals and
accolades. In team sports, the Indian men's hockey team’s bronze in the 2020 Olympics and the
Indian women’s volleyball team's silver in the BRICS Games further highlight the country’s
sporting achievements.

“India Women Volleyball team
won Silver Medal & Men
secured Bronze Medal in the
BRICS Games at
Johannesburg, South Africa.”

The Indian Women’s Volleyball team
achieved a remarkable milestone by
winning the Silver Medal, while the Men’s
team secured the Bronze Medal at the
BRICS Games held in Johannesburg,
South Africa. This achievement highlights
the growing strength of Indian volleyball
on the international stage and showcases
the dedication and talent of the athletes.
Their impressive performances at the
prestigious event not only earned them
podium finishes but also brought pride to
the nation.


“2018 Commonwealth Games,
26 Gold, 20 Silver and 20
Bronze”

At the 2018 Commonwealth Games held in
Gold Coast, Australia, India delivered an
outstanding performance, securing a total
of 66 medals: 26 Gold, 20 Silver, and 20
Bronze. This achievement marked India's
best-ever medal haul at the
Commonwealth Games, showcasing the
country’s strength in sports like wrestling,
weightlifting, badminton, and boxing. The
impressive results highlighted the progress
of Indian athletes on the global stage and
reinforced the country's growing sporting
reputation.
