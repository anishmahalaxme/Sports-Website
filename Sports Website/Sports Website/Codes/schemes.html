NATIONAL SPORTS AWARDS

Arjuna Award

Dronacharya Award

Padma Shri
The Arjuna Award recognizes athletes for exceptional achievements in sports and their contribution to Indian sports. The Dronacharya Award honors coaches for their exceptional contribution to the development of athletes and sports in India. The Padma Shri is a prestigious civilian award given to athletes for their exceptional contributions to Indian sports.

Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award

Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puruskar

Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award
The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award is India's highest sporting honor, recognizing athletes for outstanding performance at the international level. The Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puruskar recognizes individuals and organizations for promoting sports and nurturing grassroots talent in India. The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award is given to athletes for their outstanding performance in international sports competitions, contributing to the nation’s sporting excellence.
